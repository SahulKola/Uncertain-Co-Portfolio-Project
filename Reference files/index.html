What I Do

ML Development

I absolutely love developing end-to-end data-driven applications. Training machines to augment human intelligence is my thing. Believe it or not, smart software is influencing our decisions more than Elon Musk.

Data Analysis

I can help make sense of the immense datastore that the internet has to offer. Collecting, cleaning, processing, visualising, analysing and ultimately deriving insights from data makes up my perfect recipe.

Research

It is safe to say that most of my interests revolve around data. Therefore, researching to make better, more efficient and smarter use of it, to solve real world problems is another skill I boast.

Motion Graphics

Providing a visual medium to express ideas is extremely empowering. Be it manipulating shapes or text, animating infographics or giving life to still images, I can do it all, and do it well.
